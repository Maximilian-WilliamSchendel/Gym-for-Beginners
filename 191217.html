Equipment

Knowing the functions of equipment and how to use them properly is the most crucial part in starting your Fitness journey. In order to avoid injury or improper use, make sure to know how to use the equipment and which form is needed for the exercises.


Muscles and Exercises

Gaining a basic knowledge of which muscle groups there are and with what exercises to target them with, is one of the core areas in the gym.


Diet

Even though doing repetitive training on your muscles is key for growth, 80% of the work is done in the kitchen. Maintaining a proper diet fitting your needs and gaining insight on what nutrition is beneficial for you, is essential for growth.
